What Is SEO and Why Is It Important for Your Business?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is a set of strategies and techniques designed to improve the visibility of a website on search engine results pages (SERPs). When someone searches for a particular product or service, businesses that have implemented strong SEO practices are more likely to appear at the top of those results. This increased visibility can result in more clicks, more website traffic, and ultimately, more conversions.

But what exactly does it take to get to that coveted spot on the search engine results page? The key is aligning your website with search engines’ ranking factors, which include relevance, authority, and user experience. That’s where an SEO agency comes into play. They help you ensure that your website checks all the boxes that search engines like Google are looking for.
